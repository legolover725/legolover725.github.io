The Kabul

Description:
An authentic Afghan Cuisine that specializes in Marinated kebabs, cooked over open flame. we specialize in kebabs, afghan style variety of vegetarian dishes, such as kadu-pumpkin and fresh baked tandoori naan. To spice up things we offer signature bowls, specialty curries that will take you on a journey of real Afghan food flavors. we make everything in our kitchen, no preservatives, no added colors, only best ingredients with fresh flavors. we buy local produce and poultry. The reason for our success is quality food made fresh right there and then.
